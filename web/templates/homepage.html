How It Works

Simple, efficient, and privacy-focused emergency detection

1

Sound Detection

ESP32 with a sound sensor continuously monitor rooms for emergency sounds without recording audio.

2

Edge Processing

Audio frames are converted to mel spectrograms and analyzed by an ML model on the Raspberry Pi hub.

3

Smart Analysis

CNN model classifies sounds with sophisticated thresholding to prevent false alarms.

4

Instant Alert

Visual and audio alerts activate for the specific room, logged in the database.
